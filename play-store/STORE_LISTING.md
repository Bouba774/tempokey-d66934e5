## Full description (≤4000 chars)

```
TempoKey is the offline DJ companion that turns any folder of audio files into
a fully analyzed, mixable library.

Point TempoKey at your music folder and let it compute BPM, musical key,
Camelot notation, energy and duration for every track — entirely on your
device. No account. No upload. No cloud.

▸ PRECISE ANALYSIS
  • BPM detection with confidence scoring
  • Key & Camelot wheel notation
  • Energy estimation
  • Per-track confidence indicators so you know what to trust

▸ HARMONIC MIXING
  • Suggests perfectly compatible next tracks
  • Color-coded compatibility badges
  • Camelot wheel visualization
  • Set Builder to craft full DJ sets

▸ LIBRARY MANAGEMENT
  • Massive imports (10 000+ tracks)
  • Smart filters & search
  • Duplicate detection
  • Intelligent batch renaming with safe history & rollback

▸ DJ DASHBOARD
  • Visual overview of your library: BPM distribution, key spread, energy map
  • Spot dead zones and unlock new transitions at a glance

▸ PROFESSIONAL UI
  • Premium Android design, dark by default
  • Snappy on phones and tablets
  • Works fully offline

▸ PRIVACY FIRST
  • All analysis runs locally on your device
  • Your audio files never leave your phone
  • Zero analytics by default — opt-in only
  • No ads, no tracking, no account

TempoKey is built for DJs, producers and serious music collectors who want a
fast, accurate and respectful way to understand and organize their library.
```

### Capturing screenshots

Run the app in the Android emulator (or a connected device) and capture the
following flows in this order — they map 1:1 to the marketing narrative:

1. **Library** — populated track list with BPM, Camelot and energy badges.
2. **Track detail** — analysis panel with confidence indicators.
3. **Harmonic Mixing** — suggestions for a currently playing track.
4. **Set Builder** — a built set with compatibility chips.
5. **DJ Dashboard** — BPM / key distribution charts.
6. **Smart Renaming** — preview of a batch rename with diff.

For tablets, capture #1 and #5 in landscape.
